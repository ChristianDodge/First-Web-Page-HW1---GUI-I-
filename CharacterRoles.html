This page shows how each character is tiered in the tier list. DPS characters are characters that specialize in dealing damage. Healer-shielder characters specialize in healing the teammates, or shielding the teammates. Support characters specialize in providing some kinds of stat increases/buffs to teammates. Sub-DPS characters specialize in dealing moderate damage, but at not the primary source of damage for the team they are in. Characters with an asterisk (*) also denote that they are good at off-field (or on-field in some cases, such as Tartaglia's role in his ideal teams) elemental application (for whatever element said character utilizes), but only for non-DPS characters (as they practically all apply their element constantly/easily while on-field).
